About Me

I am a researcher and aspiring PhD candidate in Behavioral Health and Epidemiology, with a focus on employment conditions, migrant health, and the intersection of AI with public health research. I joined the Generative AI for Work & Research course to enhance my ability to integrate no-code AI tools into research and data analysis workflows.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research is an 8-week live cohort course by GRAPH Courses, focused on applying no-code AI tools for task automation, data analysis, and research communication. The course features live workshops, project coaching, and a capstone showcase.

Key modules include:

  • Websites, Documents & Presentations
  • AI-Automated Data Analysis
  • GenAI for Research & Text Analysis
  • Custom GPTs and Chatbots
  • AI for Task Automation
  • GenAI for Communication and Outreach
