About the Course

An 8‑week live cohort that blends no‑code AI tools with practical, research‑ready workflows. Features weekly workshops, help sessions, a supportive community, personalized feedback, and a capstone showcase.

  • Weekly live workshops & help sessions
  • Automations, dashboards, custom GPTs & chatbots
  • Structured text & research tooling (Markdown, HTML)
  • Automated reports, spreadsheets, and visualizations
  • Final project coaching & a shareable certificate

Grading: Quizzes (15%), Workshops (35%), Participation (10%), Final Project (40%) • Passing: 80%

Module Snapshot

  • Websites, Documents & Presentations – LLM basics; generate structured outputs; build simple research sites.
  • AI‑Automated Data Analysis – Code Interpreter/Julius; transform datasets; automated reporting & visuals.
  • GenAI for Research & Text Analysis – Literature search, classification, sentiment, knowledge maps.
  • Custom GPTs & Chatbots – RAG concepts; Botpress; research assistants with knowledge bases.
  • AI for Task Automation – Agents, email flows, browser control for repetitive tasks.
  • Communication & Outreach – Summaries, figures, video abstracts, visualization prompts.
  • Capstone & Showcase – Project development, peer feedback, public presentation.
